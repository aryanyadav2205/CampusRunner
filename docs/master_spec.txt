Project Name
Campus Runner
Tagline
Someone's already going. Let them bring yours too.
Project Overview
Campus Runner is a peer-to-peer parcel pickup and delivery platform for college campuses.
Students who are already visiting the campus parcel collection center can earn money by collecting and
delivering parcels for other students.
The platform acts as a trusted marketplace between parcel owners and student runners.
Primary Goals
Reduce inconvenience for students who cannot visit the parcel center .
Allow students to earn money by delivering parcels.
Create a secure and trusted delivery ecosystem.
Support both prepaid and COD orders.
Provide transparent ratings and reputation.
